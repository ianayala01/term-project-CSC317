General Questions

What is Travel Essentials?

Travel Essentials is an online store specializing in high-quality travel and lifestyle products. We offer a curated selection of items that make travel more comfortable, convenient, and enjoyable, from travel bags and electronics to comfort accessories and organizational tools.

How can I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team by:

  • Email: ritesh@sfsu.edu
  • Phone: +1 (669) 649-1071, available Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6 PM EST
  • Contact Form: Fill out the form on our Contact page

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days.

Do you have a physical store?

Currently, Travel Essentials operates exclusively online. This allows us to offer a wider range of products at competitive prices and serve customers worldwide. However, we occasionally participate in travel expos and pop-up shops. Follow our social media accounts for announcements about these events.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping rates and delivery times vary depending on your location. You can see the exact shipping costs during checkout after entering your address.

Products

How do you select your products?

All products on our site are carefully selected by our team of travel enthusiasts. We personally test each item to ensure it meets our standards for quality, functionality, durability, and value. We prioritize products that solve common travel problems and enhance the travel experience.

Are your products covered by warranty?

Most of our products come with a manufacturer's warranty, typically ranging from 90 days to 1 year, depending on the item. The specific warranty information is listed on each product page. Additionally, Travel Essentials offers a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all purchases.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer gift wrapping services for a small additional fee. During checkout, you'll have the option to add gift wrapping to your order. You can also include a personalized message that will be printed on a gift card.

Are your products TSA-approved?

Many of our travel accessories are designed to comply with TSA regulations. However, security regulations can change, and they may vary by country and airport. We recommend checking the latest TSA guidelines before traveling. For products like toiletry bottles, luggage locks, and electronics, we specifically mention TSA compliance in the product description.

Shipping & Delivery

What are your shipping rates?

Our shipping rates vary based on your location and the order value:

  • US Domestic: Free standard shipping on orders over $50, otherwise $5.95
  • Canada: $9.95 standard shipping, free for orders over $75
  • International: Starting at $14.95, varies by country

Expedited shipping options are available at checkout for an additional fee.

How long will it take to receive my order?

Delivery times depend on your location and the shipping method selected:

  • US Domestic: 3-5 business days for standard shipping, 1-2 business days for expedited
  • Canada: 5-7 business days
  • International: 7-14 business days, depending on the destination

Please note that these are estimated delivery times and may vary due to customs processing, weather conditions, or other factors beyond our control.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to track your package. You can also view your order status and tracking information by logging into your account and navigating to the "Order History" section.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all purchases. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Items must be returned in their original condition and packaging, with all tags attached.

Some exceptions apply to items marked as "Final Sale" or personalized products.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return:

  1. Log into your account and go to "Order History"
  2. Select the order containing the item(s) you wish to return
  3. Click "Return Items" and follow the prompts
  4. Print the return shipping label (if provided)
  5. Package the item(s) securely with the original packaging
  6. Attach the return label and drop off at the specified carrier

If you don't have an account or need assistance, please contact our customer service team.

Who pays for return shipping?

For returns due to a defect, incorrect item, or our error, we'll cover the return shipping costs. For returns due to personal preference, sizing issues, or other non-defect reasons, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs. A return shipping label can be purchased during the return process, with the cost deducted from your refund amount.

Account & Orders

Do I need an account to place an order?

While you can checkout as a guest, we recommend creating an account for a better shopping experience. With an account, you can:

  • Track your orders and view order history
  • Save multiple shipping addresses
  • Speed up future checkouts
  • Receive exclusive offers and promotions
  • Create wishlists

Creating an account is quick, free, and only requires an email address and password.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Click "Reset Password"
  5. Check your email for a password reset link
  6. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

The password reset link expires after 24 hours for security reasons. If you don't receive the email, please check your spam folder or contact our customer service.

Do you offer a loyalty program?

Yes, we offer a Travel Rewards program. For every purchase, you earn points that can be redeemed for discounts on future orders. Additionally, members receive exclusive offers, early access to new products, and birthday rewards. You're automatically enrolled in the program when you create an account.

Points structure:

  • 1 point for every $1 spent
  • 100 points = $5 discount
  • Double points on your birthday month
  • Bonus points for reviews and social media shares
